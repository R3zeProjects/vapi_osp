ExportVersionWindows - export checklist
=======================================

Platform: Windows (DLL)

Root: README.md, CONTACT.txt, EXPORT_CHECKLIST.txt
licenses/: LICENSE_VAPI.txt, LICENSE_GLFW.txt, LICENSE_VULKAN.txt, README.txt
docs/: README.md, 01-overview.md ... 06-worksheet.md
examples/: main.c, README.md

bin/ (filled from dll/ by vapi_export_copy when VAPI_BUILD_DLL=ON):
  [x] VAPI DLL (libvapi_shared.dll or vapi.dll)
  [x] vapi_c_api.hpp
  [x] Import library (vapi.lib or libvapi_shared.dll.a)
  [x] shaders/ (ui.vert.spv, ui.frag.spv)
  [ ] vulkan-1.dll  - Copied from: VULKAN_SDK Bin/Bin32, or VULKAN_LOADER_DLL, or %SystemRoot%\System32 or SysWOW64. Required at runtime.
  [x] glfw3.dll     - Copied only from VAPI_GLFW_DIR (no static/import lib in bin/)
  [x] README.txt, HOWTO_USE.md

Before publishing: ensure vulkan-1.dll is in bin/ (build with VULKAN_SDK or so system copy is used, or add manually). Do not distribute .git or .idea.
