--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

When contacting the Creator to request an Agreement for commercial use, please
state clearly:

  — Purpose (goal) of use: why and for what you need the Software (e.g. to
    build an internal tool, to ship a desktop application, to run a
    commercial service). Implementation or product details are not required—
    only the intended goal of use.

  — Type of use: in what capacity the Software will be used (e.g. as part of
    a product you distribute, internal use only, integration into a
    customer-facing service).

  — Term (duration): the period for which you need the Agreement (e.g. one
    year, indefinite until terminated, specific end date).

This information allows the Creator to prepare an Agreement Document with the
appropriate scope and conditions. The Creator does not need to know the
technical or business implementation details of your product—only the purpose
and type of use.
